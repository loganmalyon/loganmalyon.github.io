Logan

Hey, I'm Logan. I am a freelance artist based in London. A self taught painter, exploring a multitude of mediums. I have used art to express myself and connect to the world around me ever since I can remember.

My main focus is lino printing, gouache and acrylic. I am drawn to bold colours and expressive pieces invoking emotional responses.

Finding this world to be a huge and wildly confusing place to inhabit I find that what I create enables me to feel more integrated within the chaos that surrounds us rather than being a spectator.
